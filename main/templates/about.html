About Us

"Empowering Communities Through
Martial Arts and Boxing."
Our Community Mission

To make martial arts and boxing a cornerstone of our community, inspiring the younger generation while promoting discipline, confidence, and personal growth. We aim to keep kids off the streets and away from harmful influences, building champions both in the ring and in life.

Our Fitness Goal

To help every individual—whether seeking self-defense, improved fitness, weight loss, or competitive excellence—achieve their personal best through world-class training in our state-of-the-art facility. Wolfhouse is committed to shaping a healthier, stronger, and more connected community.

Welcome to Wolfhouse

At Wolfhouse, we’re more than just a gym—we’re a community driven by passion, purpose, and perseverance. Based in Edmonton, we specialize in providing world-class martial arts, boxing, and fitness training in a state-of-the-art facility. Whether you’re a beginner, a seasoned athlete, or just looking for a new challenge, Wolfhouse is the place to grow, train, and thrive.

Why Choose Wolfhouse?
  • Expert Coaching: Our experienced team includes top-level coaches and active fighters passionate about helping you succeed.
  • Inclusive Community: We foster an environment where everyone feels welcome and supported, no matter their background.
  • Comprehensive Training: With programs in boxing, Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu- Jitsu, wrestling, and MMA, we provide well-rounded development for all. Join the Pack
Our dedication
  • Creating Champions: From novices to pros, we help individuals achieve their personal and competitive goals.
  • Building Character: Teaching discipline, respect, and confidence through combat sports.
  • Inspiring the Youth: Keeping kids off the streets and away from negative influences by offering positive outlets and mentorship.
Our Programs

At Wolfhouse, we cater to all ages and experience levels. From beginner classes and kids’ programs to advanced training for professionals, we focus on skill development, fitness, and self-defense in a safe and inclusive environment.

Join the Pack

Wolfhouse isn’t just a gym—it’s a family. Together, we rise, overcome challenges, and celebrate victories, big or small. Whether you’re here to get fit, learn self-defense, or compete at the highest level, Wolfhouse is here to guide you every step of the way.
